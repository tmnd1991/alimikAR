Alimik

One of Italy’s youngest and most creative animation Studios, AliMik combines talented artists from all around the globe in an innovative structure that produces stunning high quality animation projects of any kind.

The idea behind Alimik is to bring together the diverse and dynamic skills that our young team of professionals has acquired and matured during their experiences in different countries and continents.

The joint effort of our 3d animators and graphic designers enables us to augment and vary the line of products in order to meet all of our client’s needs.

From virtual reality applications for the web to architectural and design videos or rendering; and not only, our expertise is the collaboration with specialized staff in mechanical engineering, a prime sector of our territory, which makes us one of the few companies that can develop 3d industrial videos from simple 2d models in complete autonomy that are mechanically exact and can therefore be used not only for advertising purposes.
